Who are we?

We are a modern family living in this modern world. We are known for being rather rambunctious and quirky. Perhaps a good way to go about this is to imagine mixing all the flaws of the Goldberg's, a pinch of the drama of the Kardashian's, and the idiocy of the criminals in Home Alone 3.

Usually, we are just trying to make the most of the situation.
